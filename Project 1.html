Welcome to BGC

BGC

Fort Bonifacio Development Corporation (FBDC) is the master planner of Bonifacio Global City. FBDC is a merger of Bases Conversion Development Authority (BCDA), Ayala Land, Inc. (ALI) and Evergreen Holdings, Inc. As the developer of BGC, FBDC owns several office and retail properties across the estate – including Bonifacio High Street, The Flats BGC, Clock In BGC and the Philippine Stock Exchange Tower. BGC is a ‘city that works’, under its wing are several subsidiaries that run the city 24/7. As a city with a soul, BGC also has its own corporate social responsibility arm, and an art foundation that looks out for its public art pieces, The Mind Museum and the BGC Arts Center.

Top Spot

The Mind Museum

The Mind Museum at BGC in Taguig is an interactive science museum designed to inspire curiosity through over 250 exhibits across themes like Atom, Life, Earth, Universe, and Technology. Highlights include a life-sized T-Rex skeleton, an earthquake simulator, and a mini planetarium. Opened in 2012 as the Philippines' first world-class science museum, it offers workshops, lectures, and rotating exhibits, making it a popular educational hub in the heart of BGC.

Rockwell

Rockwell at BGC in Taguig is a premium residential and commercial development by Rockwell Land, known for its luxurious amenities and modern design. Nestled in Bonifacio Global City, it offers high-end condominium residences, office spaces, and curated retail areas. Rockwell at BGC combines convenience with exclusivity, featuring beautifully landscaped spaces, fitness centers, pools, and easy access to BGC’s bustling lifestyle hubs. Its design emphasizes privacy and comfort, making it an ideal choice for those seeking upscale urban living in one of Metro Manila's most dynamic areas.

UptownMall

Uptown Mall in BGC, Taguig, is a premium lifestyle and shopping destination developed by Megaworld Corporation. Known for its upscale atmosphere, the mall features a mix of high-end fashion brands, global dining options, and entertainment venues, including a state-of-the-art cinema. Its modern design incorporates outdoor spaces, a grand fountain, and lush greenery, creating a relaxed, stylish ambiance. Located in the vibrant Uptown Bonifacio district, Uptown Mall is a go-to spot for shopping, dining, and leisure, appealing to both locals and visitors looking for an elevated mall experience in Metro Manila.
